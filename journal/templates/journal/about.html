About Plantly

Hello and welcome to Plantly! My name is Dana. I created this app for two reasons:

  1. 1. To make something useful
  2. 2. To teach myself Django

I love plants (see picture of me surrounded by plants.) I use this app every day to keep my house plants healthy and thriving. I developed an iOS app with similar functionality back in school, but when I decided to learn Django I felt that remaking that application would be a perfect way for me to pick up the framework. Feel free to check out my plants on the Home page, or make your own account to keep track of yours!
